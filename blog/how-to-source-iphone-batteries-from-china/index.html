How to Source iPhone Batteries from China Safely

China is a major sourcing market for iPhone battery replacement parts, but B2B buyers should use a structured process before placing large orders.

Start with a clear model list

Do not ask only for a general price list. Send the models you need, target quantity, preferred quality line, and destination country. Clear information helps the supplier quote faster and prevents wrong-model orders.

Use samples before large orders

If you are testing a new supplier, start with a sample or smaller mixed-SKU order. Check packing, labeling, model match, communication speed, and after-sales attitude before scaling.

Confirm payment details carefully

For international buyers, PayPal can be convenient for smaller orders. Some buyers prefer Alipay or bank transfer after a relationship is established. Always confirm final amount, shipping cost, and payment account in the same conversation thread.

Keep warranty terms written

A written warranty term is important for repair shops and distributors. CellCore Global lists 12 months warranty, but buyers should still confirm claim handling, photos needed, and replacement or credit workflow.
