Original Cell vs Pulled Original iPhone Batteries

Wholesale buyers often ask whether they should stock original cell batteries, pulled original batteries, or both. The answer depends on repair positioning, available models, and customer expectations.

What original cell batteries are used for

Original cell batteries are a common wholesale option for repair shops that need consistent stock and predictable pricing. They are suitable for regular repair demand where the buyer wants a balance of quality, availability, and cost.

What pulled original batteries are used for

Pulled original batteries are typically sourced from original devices or assemblies. They can be attractive for buyers who specifically request original pulled stock, but availability is usually more limited and changes by model.

How repair shops choose between them

For everyday volume repairs, original cell stock may be easier to plan. For certain premium or special customer requests, pulled original stock can be useful. Many B2B buyers keep original cell batteries as the main line and buy pulled original stock when a specific series is available.

Questions to ask before ordering

  • Which models are currently available?
  • Is the quoted price for original cell or pulled original stock?
  • What is the warranty period?
  • What is the MOQ by model?
  • Can the supplier mix models in one order?
