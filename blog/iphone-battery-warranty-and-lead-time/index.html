iPhone Battery Warranty and Lead Time for Wholesale Buyers

Warranty and lead time affect your repair schedule, customer promises, and cash flow. Wholesale buyers should confirm both before placing an iPhone battery order.

Why lead time matters

A 5-15 day lead time gives repair shops a planning window, but final timing depends on stock, order size, packing, and shipping route. Large mixed-model orders should be confirmed before payment.

What a 12-month warranty should cover

Warranty terms should explain the period, evidence needed for claims, and whether support is handled by replacement, credit, or next-order adjustment. Ask your supplier to keep this clear in writing.

How distributors should plan stock

Distributors should separate fast-moving models from special-request models. Keep enough stock for weekly demand, then reorder before the shelf is empty. This avoids emergency shipping costs and customer delays.

Information to confirm before payment

  • Exact model names and quantities.
  • Current stock and dispatch timing.
  • Shipping method and destination country.
  • Warranty period and claim process.
  • Final PayPal, Alipay, or other payment details.
